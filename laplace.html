About This Project

This project is part of my 2026 Science and IT Exhibition, and acts as a milestone in my studies of mathematics and computer science. It demonstrates the use of Laplace transforms for solving differential equations and compares numerical methods like Euler and Runge-Kutta 4 on accuracy and performance. Below are some brief descriptions about each of the projects.

Euler Method

The Euler Method is a first-order numerical procedure for solving ODEs with given initial conditions. It approximates solutions by taking small steps along the tangent line of the curve, updating the next value based on the current slope. Taking smaller step sizes increases accuracy.
